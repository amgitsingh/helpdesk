# Knowledge Base — FAQ

This file contains frequently asked questions and answers for the support team. It covers the online learning platform, course purchases, and the LMS.

---

## Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address and we will send you a password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder.

**Q: I cannot log in even though my password is correct.**
A: First, make sure Caps Lock is off. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again. If the issue persists, try a different browser or incognito mode. If you still cannot log in, contact support with your registered email and we will investigate.

**Q: How do I change my email address?**
A: Log in and go to Account Settings > Profile. Update your email address and save. A confirmation link will be sent to the new address — you must click it to complete the change.

**Q: Can I have more than one account?**
A: No. Each person is allowed one account. Duplicate accounts may be merged or removed without notice.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Account Settings > Danger Zone and click "Delete Account". This is permanent and will remove all your course progress and purchase history. If you have an active subscription, cancel it first.

## Course Access & Enrollment

**Q: I purchased a course but I cannot access it.**
A: After purchase, the course should appear in your dashboard immediately. Try refreshing the page or logging out and back in. If it still does not appear after 10 minutes, contact support with your order confirmation number.

**Q: How long do I have access to a course after purchasing?**
A: Lifetime access — unless stated otherwise on the course page. You can return to the course and watch the content at any time.

**Q: Can I access courses on mobile?**
A: Yes. The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app is not currently available.

**Q: I enrolled in a free course but it is now asking me to pay.**
A: Free enrollment grants access at the time of enrollment. If a course moved from free to paid after you enrolled, your original access should still be active. Contact support if you are being incorrectly blocked.

**Q: Can I share my course access with someone else?**
A: No. Courses are licensed to a single account and cannot be shared or transferred.

## Video Playback & LMS

**Q: A video is buffering or not loading.**
A: Check your internet connection first. Try lowering the video quality using the quality selector in the player. If the problem affects all videos, clear your browser cache or switch browsers. Videos require a stable connection of at least 5 Mbps for HD playback.

**Q: The video player shows an error and will not start.**
A: Disable any browser extensions (especially ad blockers or VPNs) and try again. Make sure your browser is up to date. If the issue persists on multiple browsers, report it to support with the course name and the error message shown.

**Q: My progress is not being saved.**
A: Progress is saved automatically when you complete a lesson. Make sure you do not close the tab mid-lesson. If progress is consistently not saving, log out, clear your cache, and log back in. Contact support if the problem continues.

**Q: Can I download videos for offline viewing?**
A: Offline downloads are not supported at this time. All content must be streamed through the platform.

**Q: The subtitles / captions are missing or out of sync.**
A: Not all courses include captions. If captions are listed as available on the course page but are not showing, toggle captions off and on in the player. If they remain out of sync, report it to support with the course name and lesson title.

**Q: I completed a lesson but it is not marked as complete.**
A: A lesson is marked complete only when you reach the end of the video. Skipping ahead may prevent it from registering. Watch the full video and the lesson should update automatically. If not, wait a few minutes and refresh.

## Certificates

**Q: How do I get my certificate of completion?**
A: Complete all lessons in the course. Your certificate will be generated automatically and will appear in your profile under "My Certificates". You can download it as a PDF or share a link.

**Q: My certificate shows the wrong name.**
A: Certificates use the full name set in your account profile. Go to Account Settings > Profile, update your name, and then regenerate the certificate from the "My Certificates" page.

**Q: I completed the course but my certificate has not appeared.**
A: Make sure every lesson is marked complete — check the course curriculum for any unchecked lessons. If all lessons are complete and the certificate is still missing after 15 minutes, contact support.

## Payments & Refunds

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. Payment is processed securely; we do not store card details.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all courses, no questions asked. To request a refund, contact support with your order number and the email address used at checkout.

**Q: I was charged but did not receive access to the course.**
A: This can happen if there was a payment processing delay. Wait up to 15 minutes and refresh your dashboard. If you still do not have access, contact support with your order confirmation email and we will resolve it immediately.

**Q: My payment was declined.**
A: Check that your card details and billing address are entered correctly. Contact your bank to ensure the transaction is not being blocked. Try a different card or payment method. If the issue continues, contact support.

**Q: I see an unexpected charge on my statement.**
A: Check your purchase history in Account Settings > Billing. If the charge does not match any purchase, contact support immediately with the charge amount and date.

**Q: Do you offer discounts or coupons?**
A: Yes. Coupon codes can be applied at checkout. Promotional discounts are announced via email and on our website. Coupons cannot be applied retroactively after a purchase is completed.

## Subscriptions

**Q: What is included in a subscription plan?**
A: Subscription plans grant unlimited access to all courses in the catalog for the duration of the subscription. Individual lifetime purchases are separate and unaffected by subscription status.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Account Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription and click "Cancel Subscription". Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for unused subscription time.

**Q: My subscription renewed but I wanted to cancel.**
A: If the renewal happened within the last 48 hours, contact support and we will review your case for a refund.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Subscription pausing is not currently supported. You can cancel and resubscribe when you are ready.

## Technical & Browser Support

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: The platform supports the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported.

**Q: The platform looks broken or unstyled.**
A: Hard-refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) to clear cached assets. If the issue persists, try a different browser.

**Q: I am getting a "session expired" message.**
A: Sessions expire after a period of inactivity for security. Log back in and your progress will be intact.

**Q: Does the platform support screen readers?**
A: We are working toward full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact support and we will prioritise a fix.
